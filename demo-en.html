Example of Social Share Privacy in English

(with Facebook "like" button)

2 Clicks for better privacy

The idea behind the "+2" concept is that unless you actively decide to recommend a page via a social network, there's no reason why the social network itself should be able to track your visit of a page.

The Social Share Privacy widget is maintained by the German magazine Heise Online, so the original language is German, but it's not hard to internationalize (just view the source of this page). For once, English speakers get to feel the taste of i18n :)
